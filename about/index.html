I am an artist born and raised in Lubbock, Texas, where I currently reside.

I graduated from Texas Tech University in 2010 with a BFA in Painting and Drawing. Though i still enjoy drawing, I've found work and inspiration in photography.

My work mainly focuses on the human form, a subject I grew attached to after taking life drawing classes at Texas Tech.

I would like to thank Flatlands Dance Theatre and Emma Foster for their help in bringing my art and vision to life.
